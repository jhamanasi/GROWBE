Understanding PITI

PITI stands for Principal, Interest, Taxes, and Insurance.

Components of PITI:

  • Principal: This is the portion of your monthly payment that goes toward reducing the original amount borrowed.
  • Interest: This is the cost of borrowing the money, expressed as a percentage of the loan amount.
  • Taxes: Property taxes are assessed by local governments.
  • Insurance: This includes homeowners insurance and private mortgage insurance.
